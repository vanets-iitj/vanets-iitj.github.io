  • Invited Talk, Arijit Karati, 2025

    This talk will explore specific authentication solutions in vehicular communication and smart card application scenarios. We will analyze methods leveraging both classical cryptographic primitives and Physically Unclonable Functions (PUFs) to establish secure, hardware-rooted trust. The discussion will conclude by covering the implications of emerging threats, specifically addressing post-quantum authentication aspects and some ongoing design considerations for resilient remote data authentication systems.
