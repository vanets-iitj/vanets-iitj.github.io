Seminars and Meetings



  • BRICS Young Scientist Award, 2022

    Ankur Nahar received BRICS Young Scientist Award, 2022 in the area of Artificial Intelligence (AI) from BRICS Young Scientist Forum Conclave - 2022, National Institute of Advanced Studies, Govt. of India.

    The forum provides a high-level academic and policy exchange platform for outstanding young scientists and scientific and technological talents from BRICS countries, promoting exchanges and cooperation in research and development.

    List of Candidates | Contents



  • Prof. Halimjon Khujamatov Visit

    Prof. Halimjon Khujamatov, and his associate Ernazar, from Tashkent University of Information Technologies, Uzbekistan visited VANET lab for a Indo-Uzeb collaborative project with Dr. Debasis Das at Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Jodhpur on 16th December 2021.



  • 7-day SERB Karyashala

    VANET Lab members attended a 7-day SERB Karyashala “Vehicular Communications for Next-Generation Intelligent Transportation Systems” held at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore) from 13th July 2022 to 19th July 2022.

  • Prof. Sajal K. Das Visit

    Prof. Sajal K. Das, Professor and Daniel St. Clair Endowed Chair, Department of Computer Science from Missouri University of Science and Technology Rolla, MO 65409, USA gives a tech talk on Merely Fun With Algorithms at Computer Science and Engineering Department, IIT Jodhpur on 5th January 2022.

  • Indo-Taiwan collaborative project

    Dr. Arijit Karati, from National Sun Yat-sen University (NSYSU), Kaohsiung, Taiwan, visited the VANET lab for an Indo-Taiwan collaborative project with Dr. Debasis Das at Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Jodhpur on 2nd August 2022.

  • Students visit from different schools

    Students from different schools of Jodhpur visited VANET lab to learn about the intelligent transportation systems and technology advancements at Computer Science and Engineering Department at IIT Jodhpur on 27th August 2022.
