Meet the Team!

Dr. Debasis Das

Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering(CSE), IIT Jodhpur

Dr. Debasis Das is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering and Associate Faculty at the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AIDE) as well as Head of the Computer Center (Data Center) and Co-ordinator of Center for AIOT and Applications (Inter-disciplinary Research Platform (IDRP)) at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India. He joined IIT Jodhpur in 2019, and before joining IIT Jodhpur, he was working as a Faculty member at NIIT University, India, and BITS Pilani, K.K. Birla Goa campus, Goa, India. His research interests include Vehicular Ad-Hoc Networks (VANETs), Blockchain, Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning (ML), and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Dr. Das published 150 research papers in top international journals (i.e., IEEE Transactions, ACM transactions etc) and top Conferences (Like A/A* Conferences). Dr. Das has 20 sponsored projects and 2 consultancy projects to his credit. He has been awarded the Early Career Research (ECR) Award and the BRICS Young Scientist Award from the Science & Engineering Research Board (SERB), Department of Science & Technology, Govt. of India. He has many research collaborations with premier institutions in India and different universities across the globe. He has been engaged in different academic activities inside and outside the Institute. He has supervised 5 Ph.D. students, and 8 are currently pursuing their thesis. He has also supervised more than 50 M.E./M.Tech Thesis. He is a senior member of IEEE and VTS and a professional member of ACM.
